Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

BuyNTrade respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect and how we use it.

  • Account data: We collect your username, email address, and password (stored securely) when you register.
  • Activity data: We store listings, bids, comments, favorites, and community interactions linked to your account.
  • Usage data: We may collect basic technical information such as browser type and pages visited to improve the service.
  • How we use data: To operate the marketplace, display your activity, prevent abuse, and improve user experience.
  • Sharing: We do not sell your personal information. Public listing and bid information is visible to other users as part of normal platform use.
  • Your choices: You may request account deletion by contacting privacy@buyntrade.co.za.

Cookie Policy

Last updated: July 2026

BuyNTrade uses cookies and similar technologies to keep the site working properly.

  • Essential cookies: Required for login sessions, security (CSRF protection), and core site functionality.
  • Preference cookies: We store your theme choice (dark or light mode) in your browser’s local storage.
  • Analytics: If enabled in the future, analytics cookies would help us understand how the site is used. We will update this policy before introducing them.

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling essential cookies may prevent some features from working correctly.

Community Guidelines

BuyNTrade is built on trust. Everyone using the platform — buyers, sellers, and community members — should follow these standards:

  • Be respectful in comments, community spotlight posts, and all interactions.
  • Do not post spam, scams, hate speech, harassment, or misleading content.
  • Only list legal items you are authorised to sell or trade.
  • Do not manipulate bids, use fake accounts, or interfere with other users’ listings.
  • Report content that violates these guidelines to support@buyntrade.co.za.

Content or accounts that break these guidelines may be removed without notice. Repeated violations can result in permanent suspension.

Thank you for helping make BuyNTrade a safe and welcoming marketplace for everyone.
