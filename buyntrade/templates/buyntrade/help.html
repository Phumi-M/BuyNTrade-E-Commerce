Support Center

Need help with your account, a listing, or a bid? Our support resources are here to guide you.

  • Account issues: Problems logging in, registering, or updating your profile.
  • Listing help: Selling items, adding images, choosing a category, or closing a sale.
  • Bidding help: Understanding current prices, minimum bids, and favorites.
  • Community: Questions about spotlight posts, comments, or platform behaviour.

Email us at support@buyntrade.co.za and include your username, listing link (if relevant), and a clear description of the issue. We aim to respond within 1–2 business days.

Safety Tips

Your safety matters. Follow these guidelines when buying or selling on BuyNTrade:

  • Meet in public, well-lit places when exchanging items in person.
  • Never share passwords, banking PINs, or one-time codes with other users.
  • Inspect items carefully before completing a trade or payment.
  • Use traceable payment methods when possible and keep receipts.
  • Be cautious of deals that seem too good to be true or users who rush you.
  • Report suspicious listings or behaviour to support@buyntrade.co.za.

BuyNTrade facilitates connections between users but does not handle physical delivery or payment processing directly. Always use your best judgement.

Buying Guide

Ready to find your next deal? Here is how to buy successfully on BuyNTrade:

  1. Search and browse the shop

    Use search, category filters on the shop page, or recommendations to find items you want.

  2. Review the listing

    Read the description, check the image, note the starting bid, and see whether bids have already been placed.

  3. Place a competitive bid

    Your bid must be at least the starting price and higher than any existing bid. You can ask questions in the comments first.

  4. Save to Favorites

    Save items you are interested in so you can return to them quickly from Favorites in the navbar.

  5. Win and arrange collection

    If you are the highest bidder when the seller closes the listing, coordinate pickup or delivery safely with the seller.

Selling Guide

Selling on BuyNTrade is straightforward. Tap Sell in the navbar and follow these steps for the best results:

  1. Create a clear title

    Use specific names like “Samsung 55-inch TV” instead of vague titles like “TV for sale.”

  2. Write an honest description

    Include condition, age, defects, and anything else a buyer should know upfront.

  3. Set a fair starting bid

    Price competitively to attract early interest while leaving room for bidding.

  4. Add clear photos

    Upload up to 8 photos directly when you sell. Listings with good images get more bids.

  5. Choose the right category

    Help buyers find your item faster by selecting the most relevant category.

  6. Respond and close

    Answer comments promptly. When you are ready to sell, close the listing so the highest bidder wins.
