About BuyNTrade

A modern marketplace for buying, selling, and trading with people in your community.

Our Story

BuyNTrade was built to make local commerce simple, transparent, and trustworthy. We noticed that many people wanted a better way to sell items they no longer needed, find great deals nearby, and even trade goods without complicated middlemen.

What started as a student e-commerce project has grown into a platform where users can sell products, place bids, save favorites, and connect through a vibrant community. Whether you are in Johannesburg, Brakpan, Cape Town, or anywhere in South Africa, BuyNTrade helps you turn unused items into value.

Our mission is to empower everyday buyers and sellers with tools that feel modern, fast, and fair — from smart search to community spotlight stories that celebrate real trades.

How It Works

  1. Create an account

    Register for free to browse the shop, place bids, and save items to your favorites.

  2. Shop or sell

    Sellers use Sell to list items with a title, description, starting bid, photos, and category. Buyers browse the shop homepage or filter by category in the sidebar.

  3. Place bids

    Found something you want? Submit a bid higher than the current price. The listing page shows the highest bid and bidder in real time.

  4. Save and comment

    Add items to Favorites to track them easily. Ask questions or share feedback through listing comments.

  5. Close and win

    When a seller closes a listing, the highest bidder wins. Both parties can then arrange safe pickup or delivery offline.
